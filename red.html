The Broken Architecture of European Energy Wholesale Markets

Having been active participants in European energy markets for over 20 years, we haven't just watched the changes - we lived through the entire evolution of this complex landscape...

The End of Energy Market Equilibrium

  • Capital Trap: A handful of Tier-1 players and state-backed champions dominate the order books, creating a barrier to entry that stifles real competition.
  • Counterparty Risk: Traditional energy markets often turn real gains into paper-only wins, as counterparties frequently default when prices spike.
  • Regulatory Fragility: Constant, unpredictable interventions create a fear-driven market rather than a value-driven one.
