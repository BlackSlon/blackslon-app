

# LEGAL CLASSIFICATION MEMORANDUM: IPT TOKEN (BLACKSLON SETTLEMENT ZONE)

## 1. NATURE OF THE ASSET
The IPT Token is a native Utility Token of the BlackSlon Settlement Zone (BSZ). Its primary function is to provide the holder with "Access Rights" to a closed-loop energy settlement ecosystem. It is not designed to maintain a stable value relative to any fiat currency or specific commodity in a 1:1 ratio.

## 2. UTILITY VS. ASSET-REFERENCED TOKEN (ART)
Under MiCA (Regulation EU 2023/1114), the IPT Token is classified as a Utility Token (Title II) based on the following criteria:
- **Corridor Mechanism:** Unlike ARTs, which aim for a stable "peg" to an asset, IPT fluctuates within a proprietary +/- 10% "Safety Buffer" corridor. Its value is determined by internal supply/demand dynamics within the BSZ, not by a direct claim on a physical asset.
- **Access Function:** The token serves as a "digital key" to book energy capacity and manage settlement liquidity within the protocol.
- **No Redemption Guarantee (Current Phase):** At the current stage, the token does not grant a direct, unconditional right to physical redemption of a commodity, distinguishing it from commodity-backed stablecoins.

## 3. CLOSED-LOOP ECOSYSTEM
The IPT token is accepted exclusively within the BSZ protocol. Its secondary market price is a reflection of the ecosystem's utility and "Liquidity" demand, rather than a financial instrument's yield.

## 4. COMPLIANCE STATEMENT

## STRATEGIC ROADMAP: THE EVOLUTION OF BSTZ

The development of the BlackSlon Settlement Zone (BSZ) is divided into two distinct strategic phases to ensure regulatory compliance, technical stability, and sustainable liquidity growth.

* **Asset Classification:** In Phase 1, the IPT token operates strictly as a **Utility Token** under MiCA Title II. It serves as a "Digital Key" for access to the BSZ proprietary settlement protocols.
* **Corridor Mechanism:** Implementing the +/- 10% "Safety Buffer". Pricing is determined by the BSZ internal algorithm, reflecting the supply and demand for settlement capacity within the ecosystem.

    * Reservation of virtual energy settlement slots.

    * Governance within the BSZ closed-loop environment.

**Objective:** Bridging the digital "protocol" with physical energy and gas markets (RWA).

* **Regulatory Transition:** As the protocol evolves to include physical delivery, the Issuer will engage with National Competent Authorities (NCAs) to ensure compliance with local energy trading licenses and, if necessary, transition the token classification to an Asset-Referenced Token (ART) framework, backed by 1:1 physical reserves.

### INTERNAL LIQUIDITY BACKSTOP: BaSe COLLATERALIZATION

To mitigate the risk of rising energy prices without relying on third-party Liquidity Providers, the BSZ Protocol utilizes an **Internal Hedging Mechanism** driven by the **BaSe** token.

**1. Correlation Engine:**
The pricing algorithm for the BaSe token is dynamically linked to the BlackSlon Energy Indexes. As the underlying cost of energy (gas/electricity) increases, the protocol adjusts the 'a' (Base Price) and 'b' (Sensitivity) parameters of the BaSe Bonding Curve.

**2. Solvency Guarantee:**
This mechanism ensures that the protocol's treasury (denominated in BaSe) appreciates in value proportionally to the potential liabilities of the IPT vouchers. By tying the ecosystem's core asset to the energy index, the "Liquidity Buffer" remains over-collateralized even during hyper-inflationary energy events.

**3. Demand-Driven Appreciation:**
Since the IPT vouchers require BaSe for acquisition and settlement, increased demand for energy vouchers leads to a non-linear appreciation of the BaSe token. This creates an automated "safety cushion" that protects the protocol's solvency 24/7.
